Our Mission

Welcome to the C8 team at Islington College! We are a dedicated group of students who have come together to create a website for bonsai plants. Our team is made up of individuals with a passion for design, coding, and bonsai. We have worked hard to create a user-friendly website that is filled with valuable information and resources for bonsai enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you're new to bonsai or have been practicing for years, our website is the perfect place to learn more about this fascinating hobby. From tips on how to care for your bonsai trees to information on the best tools and techniques, our website has something for everyone. We are proud of the website we have created and we hope that you will find it both informative and enjoyable. Thank you for visiting our website and we look forward to sharing our passion for bonsai with you.


Our Mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality bonsai plants and related products, while also promoting the art and tradition of bonsai cultivation. We believe that bonsai plants are not just a simple form of horticulture, but rather a form of living art that requires skill, patience, and dedication to cultivate. Our website offers a wide variety of bonsai plants, from classic Juniper and Maple varieties to more unique and exotic species, as well as all the tools and accessories you need to care for them.
