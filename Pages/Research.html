Introduction

This research aims to compare the web components used on a Bonsai website against those used on five other websites that have been researched. The purpose of this research is to identify any peculiar web components that the Bonsai website possesses more than others.

Methods

To conduct this research, the bonsai website was analyzed to identify the web components used. Five other websites were also analyzed for the same purpose. The websites were selected based on their relevance to the bonsai website and their popularity.
